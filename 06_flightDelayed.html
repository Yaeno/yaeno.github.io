INTRODUCTION

Delayed Flight in 2017

A data visualization describing the delayed flights in 30 major airports crossing USA in 2017.

The project was delivered in February 2018. I mined data by R, designed graphic by Sketch and developed interaction by D3 JS.

HOW TO DEVELOP THE STORY

Process

The project covers the data mining, graphic design and interaction development.

1. Data Mining

I obtained the airport data from Bureau of Transportation Statistics , which accommodates 3000 entries.

At the outset of the project, without any topics on top of my mind, I used R to plot the data so as to explore the hidden stories from behind.

2. Shape The Story

Plotting the data unveiled many interesting stories, but they can be stated from different perspectives.

To narrow down the scale, I decided to tell the story from a passenger's perspective and selected a topic I care most - delayed flights- to drill down to detail and plan the storylines.

After the investigation, I fixed the topic on uncovering the percentage/number of delayed flights in 30 major airports across USA and analyzing their causes.

3. Plan The Data And Its Visualization

To crack down the topic, I listed the data elements needed for the story and their possible visual representations. To build the connection between the two, I also planned the interaction ideas to form a whole complete story.
