INTRODUCTION

Communicate Information Succinctly and Efficiently

Gartner's 2020 review of the user experience of Reporting revealed a red flag. To address this challenge, a discovery team was formed in three months, including a Product Manager, lead engineer, and designers. The team collaborated closely to understand user behaviors and pain points.

CHALLENGES and SOLUTIONS

Challenge 1

Understand User Behaviors and Pain Points

Instead of purely brushing the interface, the team’s goal is to rebuild the experience. Therefore, we need to understand users.

Solution

I led 10 contextual inquiries, analysis, and ideation. As a result, three pain points were identified and prioritized:
• Lack of customization;
• The current canned reports not meeting all cases;
• Out-of-date design.

Challenge 2

Boost Visual

Visual is out-of-date and the terminologies are out of users' expectations.

Solution

To boost the visual, the team took three approaches:
• Using a consistent template across all canned reports;
• Ensuring nomenclature is meaningful;
• Ensuring data visualizations tell stories.

Challenge 3

Leverage Design and Feasibility in a Short Period of Time

We want to ship more than what the schedule allows.

Solution

To ensure quality and efficiency, the team developed a minimum lovable product (MLP) for the first release. All members of the discovery team participated in user research, templates were used for canned reports, and the 3 pillars (PM, designers, front-end engineers, and data expert) collaborated to select data and discuss their presentation.
