INTRODUCTION

Make A Start

When I joined the company, the definitions of common components were ambiguous and not organized systematically. After recognizing this gap, I initiated the “common component” discussion to reach a consensus. As the team grew, I led a squad to define, build, and document common components.

Achievements

• Led a squad to collect common component issues and discussed them from the perspectives of visual, interaction, writing, accessibility and use cases;;
• Drove the development of design principles;
• Defined a documentation template and drove writing;
• Acted as a bridge between UX and UI.
